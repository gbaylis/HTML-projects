How to make a smoothie

You want a delicious smoothie, but you are tired of spending $5 every time you want your fruit and yogurt drink fix. Ever wanted to know how to make a smoothie, without having dig through tons of sites only to find recepies you do not like?

Your search ends here. Pull your blender out of the cabinet, lay out the cutting board and start chopping fruit.
An easy recipe for making a smoothie is: fill a blender pitch 3/4 with a combination of your favorite fruits, then about 1/3 of the pitcher with either milk or orange juice.
If you wanted to make it a desert, you can add a scoop of vanilla ice cream or scoop of yogurt. Hold down the blend button for about 15 seconds and it is done.
Since the fruit is room temperature, it might me better to let the smoothie sit in the fridge over night.
